# Automate Your Builder.io Content Workflows with Our New n8n Community Node

We're excited to announce the release of our latest n8n community node: n8n-nodes-builder-io! As the team at Velocity BPA, we're constantly working to bridge the gap between powerful platforms and seamless automation, and this node does exactly that for Builder.io users.

## The Problem: Manual Content Management at Scale

If you're using Builder.io as your visual CMS, you know how powerful it is for creating and managing content. But when it comes to integrating that content into larger workflows—syncing with marketing tools, triggering notifications when content is published, or automating content updates across multiple channels—you've likely been stuck with manual processes or custom code.

That changes today.

## Introducing n8n-nodes-builder-io

Our new community node brings Builder.io's content management and publishing capabilities directly into your n8n workflows. Whether you're managing a multi-channel content strategy, automating publishing workflows, or synchronizing content across platforms, this node makes it simple.

## Key Features

The n8n-nodes-builder-io node provides comprehensive access to Builder.io's API, enabling you to:

- **Fetch and manage content** from your Builder.io spaces
- **Automate publishing workflows** by triggering actions based on content status changes
- **Sync content** across multiple platforms and channels automatically
- **Integrate with other tools** in your marketing stack through n8n's extensive node ecosystem
- **Create custom content pipelines** that match your unique business processes

The possibilities are endless when you combine Builder.io's visual content creation with n8n's workflow automation power.

## Getting Started

Installing the node is straightforward. In your n8n instance, simply run:


npm install n8n-nodes-builder-io


After installation, restart n8n, and you'll find the Builder.io node ready to use in your workflow editor.
